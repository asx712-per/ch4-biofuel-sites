Market Intelligence Engine

Global Warming Potential (GWP) Multiplier

Methane (CH4) is a highly potent greenhouse gas. By capturing it and converting it into Ethanol, we destroy the methane, preventing it from entering the atmosphere.

1 Ton of Methane = ~28 Tons of CO2e (Carbon Dioxide Equivalent)

This massive multiplier means the Carbon Credit Revenue generated by this platform vastly outpaces the raw value of the physical Ethanol produced.
